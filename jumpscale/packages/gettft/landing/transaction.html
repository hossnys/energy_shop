PROOF OF CAPACITY

The Farming Process

A TFT is created every time someone adds capacity to the ThreeFold Grid. This is rewarded to the person adding the capacity.
People earn TFT for achieving a certain uptime and as proof of capacity.We call that 'Farming'.

PROOF OF UTILIZATION

The Cultivation Process

Users and organizations pay cultivation fees (using TFT) for using compute, storage and network capacity.
They then get to develop, create, deploy or host on the ThreeFold Grid.
